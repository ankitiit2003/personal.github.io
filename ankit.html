NGO PROJECT

I aim to work with an NGO to educate and provide food to underprivileged children/women. As a member of the Salt and Pepper Club's Design team, I create posters to promote fundraisers for the Paryaas team which later give to different NGOs who need help. To better understand the real problems they face, I will communicate with the NGO, explore untapped facilities, and propose new ways to help them. Additionally, I aspire to involve women in this process for a long-lasting impact. Through collaboration and action, I am determined to make a meaningful difference in the lives of these children and their communities.
